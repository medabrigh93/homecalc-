How to Calculate Mulch

Volume = Length × Width × Depth (all in feet)

Divide by 27 to get cubic yards. One cubic yard covers about 108 sq ft at 3" depth.

Recommended Mulch Depths

  • 2" — Light coverage, best for decorative stone mulch
  • 3" — Ideal for most garden beds (weed control + moisture retention)
  • 4" — Heavy weed suppression, slopes
  • 6" — Playground areas with wood chips

Frequently Asked Questions

How much mulch do I need for a 100 sq ft bed?
At 3 inches deep, you need about 0.93 cubic yards or roughly 13 bags (2 cu ft each).
Should I buy bags or bulk mulch?
For small beds (under 3 cubic yards), bags are fine. For larger areas, bulk delivery is cheaper — usually $25–50 per cubic yard delivered.
