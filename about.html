About HomeCalc

HomeCalc is a collection of free, instant calculators built for homeowners, DIY enthusiasts, and contractors. Whether you're painting a bedroom, tiling a bathroom, or building a fence, our tools help you estimate the right amount of materials — so you buy what you need and waste less.

Why We Built This

Every home project starts with the same question: "How much do I need?" We got tired of guessing, over-buying at the hardware store, and running complicated spreadsheets. So we built simple calculators that give you answers in seconds.

What You Get

  • 10 free calculators covering paint, tile, concrete, wallpaper, flooring, grout, room area, mulch, fencing, and drywall
  • No sign-up required — just enter your measurements and get results
  • Mobile-friendly — use it on your phone right at the job site
  • Waste factors included — most calculators add extra so you don't come up short
  • 100% free — no hidden fees, no paywalls

Accuracy

Our calculators use standard industry formulas and include typical waste factors. Results are estimates meant for planning purposes. For large or complex projects, we recommend consulting a professional contractor for precise measurements.
