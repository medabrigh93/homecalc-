Fence Material Guide

Post Spacing

  • 6 ft — Heavy-duty, high wind areas
  • 8 ft — Standard for most fences
  • 10 ft — Light-duty, decorative fences

Common Picket Sizes

  • 1×4 (3.5") — Standard dog-ear picket
  • 1×6 (5.5") — Privacy fence boards
  • No gap — Full privacy fence
  • 1-2" gap — Semi-private / decorative

Frequently Asked Questions

How many fence posts for 100 feet of fence?
With standard 8 ft spacing, you need 14 posts (100 ÷ 8 = 12.5 sections, rounded up = 13 sections + 1 end post = 14).
How deep should fence posts be?
Generally 1/3 of total post length underground. For a 6 ft fence, use 8 ft posts with 2 ft buried.
