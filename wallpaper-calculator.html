How to Calculate Wallpaper

Wall Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width)

Wall Area = Perimeter × Height − (Doors × 21 sq ft) − (Windows × 15 sq ft)

Usable area per roll = Roll Width (ft) × Roll Length (ft)

Rolls = Wall Area ÷ Usable Area per Roll (rounded up)

Standard Roll Sizes

  • American Single Roll: 20.5" wide × 33 ft long ≈ 56 sq ft
  • Euro Roll: 21" wide × 33 ft long ≈ 57.75 sq ft
  • Wallpaper usually sold in double rolls — check your supplier

Frequently Asked Questions

How many rolls of wallpaper for a 12×12 room?
With 8 ft ceilings and standard rolls (20.5" × 33'), you'd need about 7–8 single rolls after subtracting doors and windows.
Should I buy extra rolls?
Yes — buy 1–2 extra rolls to account for pattern matching, mistakes, and future repairs. Dye lots can vary between batches.
